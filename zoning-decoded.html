What is Zoning?

To build a system using modular components.

Social Media Strategy

The site’s two goals are to educate and engage the public.

Information Architecture

The design of information anticipates the user’s desire to share their ideas for the city’s future and learn how to stay connected first before desiring to learn how city planning works.

Printed Media

Strategically placed media helps citizens know where current planning is taking place.

Mobile Context

Excluding images and elaborate interactions helps from marginalizing users who might otherwise avoid the site because of capped data plans and slow service.
